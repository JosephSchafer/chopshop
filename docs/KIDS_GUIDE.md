# 🎧 Sound Safari — Kid's Guide

Hi! You went out and recorded cool sounds from the world. Now it's your job to
listen to each one and decide where it goes. This is the fun part. 🎉

A grown-up will start the program and open a web page for you. Then you're the
boss of the sounds.

---

## What you'll see

A card in the middle of the screen, one sound at a time:

```
   ┌───────────────────────────────────────────┐
   │  from my_recording.wav · slice 3          │
   │                                           │
   │   ▁▂▅█▇▅▂▁    (the waveform — the picture  │
   │              of your sound)               │
   │                                           │
   │        ▶  Play sound                       │
   │                                           │
   │   My guess: 💧 water (82% sure)           │
   │                                           │
   │   💧    🔔    🪵    🥃    📄              │
   │  Water Metal Wood Glass Paper  ...        │
   │                                           │
   │   [ Give it a fun name (optional) ]       │
   │                                           │
   │   ✅ Keep it!        🗑️ Toss it           │
   │   ✂️ Trim to selection  🔗 Join with next │
   └───────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

At the top there's a **progress bar** and a counter like `12 / 80` so you can see
how many sounds are left.

---

## The four-step game

For **every** sound, do this:

### 1. 👂 Listen
Press the big **▶ Play sound** button (or tap the **spacebar**). Listen to it.
You can play it as many times as you want.

### 2. 🏷️ Pick where it goes
The computer makes a **guess** (like "💧 water"). If the guess is right, great!
If it's wrong, just **tap the correct emoji** below. The one you pick lights up
with a yellow border.

The categories are things like:

| | | | | |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 💧 Water | 🔔 Metal | 🪵 Wood | 🥃 Glass | 📄 Paper |
| 🧴 Plastic | 🗣️ Voice | 👄 Mouth | 🐾 Animal | 🐦 Bird |
| 🍃 Nature | 🚗 Vehicle | 🍳 Kitchen | 🚪 Door | 🛎️ Bell |
| 🪚 Scrape | 💥 Hit | 🐭 Squeak | 🌾 Rustle | 🧸 Toy |

### 3. ✏️ Name it (if you want)
Type a fun name in the box, like **"My First Splash"** or **"Spooky Door"**.
This is optional — skip it if you want.

### 4. ✅ Keep it or 🗑️ Toss it
- Love the sound? Press **✅ Keep it!** (or tap **Enter**).
- Boring, or just noise? Press **🗑️ Toss it**.

Either way, the next sound pops up automatically. Keep going! 🚀

---

## Fixing a messy sound 🛠️

Sometimes a sound gets cut weird — maybe it has a long quiet part, or two sounds
got stuck together. You can fix it!

### ✂️ Trim — keep just the good part
1. **Drag your mouse across the waveform** to highlight the part you want to
   keep (it turns yellow).
2. Press **✂️ Trim to selection**.
3. The sound gets trimmed and plays again so you can check it.

### 🔗 Join with next — glue two pieces together
If a sound got chopped into two pieces, press **🔗 Join with next** to stick it
back together with the one right after it. (This only works if they came from the
same recording.)

---

## Keyboard shortcuts (for speed!) ⌨️

| Key | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Spacebar** | Play the sound |
| **Enter** | Keep it! |

## When you're all done 🎉

When you've gone through every sound, you'll see a happy screen:

```
   🎉 All done!
   You kept 47 sounds and tossed 12.
   Tell a grown-up to run the build step
   to put them in Ableton!
```

Go tell the grown-up — they'll press a button and your sounds become real
instruments you can play in music software. **You made an instrument out of
sounds you found in the world.** How cool is that? 🎹✨

---

## Tips 💡

- **It's okay to toss sounds.** A smaller pile of great sounds beats a huge pile
  of so-so ones.
- **Trust your ears, not the guess.** The computer is usually right, but you're
  the human — you decide.
- **Take breaks.** Your progress saves automatically, so you can stop any time
  and come back later.
